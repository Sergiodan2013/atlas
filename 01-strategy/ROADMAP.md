# Atlas Roadmap

## Phase 0 — Repository foundation

Status: in progress

Outputs:

- repository structure;
- templates;
- decision log;
- scoring model;
- initial market taxonomy.

## Phase 1 — Market map

Goal: map the AI Business Operations opportunity space.

Outputs:

- category tree;
- subcategory taxonomy;
- 100+ initial opportunity nodes;
- first opportunity scoring pass.

## Phase 2 — Competitor database

Goal: identify direct and adjacent competitors.

Outputs:

- competitor profiles;
- pricing models;
- positioning analysis;
- user pain signals;
- weaknesses and market gaps.

## Phase 3 — OSS database

Goal: identify open-source building blocks that reduce MVP development time.

Outputs:

- OSS project profiles;
- license analysis;
- maturity/activity review;
- commercial-use risk;
- build-vs-adopt recommendation.

## Phase 4 — Opportunity scoring

Goal: rank opportunities using a consistent scoring model.

Scoring dimensions:

- market pain;
- willingness to pay;
- launch speed;
- OSS readiness;
- PLG potential;
- infrastructure cost;
- competition intensity;
- expansion potential;
- defensibility;
- founder-independent execution feasibility.

## Phase 5 — Top 3 MVP candidates

Goal: choose three product candidates for validation.

Outputs:

- landing positioning;
- demo concept;
- MVP scope;
- pricing test;
- acquisition channel test;
- validation plan.

## Phase 6 — First validation build

Goal: build the smallest public experience with a clear wow moment.

Target:

> Upload a business document and receive clean structured data immediately.

Possible product direction:

- document-to-data;
- invoice extraction;
- contract extraction;
- business PDF parser;
- AI operations API.
