# Atlas OS

Atlas OS is a structured research and product-discovery operating system for finding, scoring, and launching AI business operations products.

The repository is designed to work as:

- a Git-backed knowledge base;
- an Obsidian vault;
- a product discovery workspace;
- a future AI/RAG-ready research database;
- a decision log for startup strategy.

## Current mission

Identify the strongest AI business opportunity that can be launched quickly using mature open-source software, product-led growth, and low initial infrastructure cost.

The current strategic direction is **AI Business Operations**, with special focus on document processing, invoice workflows, business data extraction, automation layers, and vertical AI operations.

## Operating principles

1. Do not start from a solution. Start from market pain.
2. Prefer atomic high-frequency business operations over broad AI platforms.
3. Score opportunities before building.
4. Use open-source infrastructure where it reduces time-to-market.
5. Avoid building heavy SaaS before validating demand.
6. Keep every important decision documented.
7. Treat this repository as the source of truth.
